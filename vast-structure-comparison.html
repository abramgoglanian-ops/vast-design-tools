The one open question
Should each team's ongoing work get its own top-level folder? Rebecca: yes. Original: no. The blend says yes, with a firm rule that anything cross-team or time-bound always lives in Programs & Initiatives. That keeps team homes visible without letting shared work fragment.
How the blend files things (the new rule, in order)
1. Cross-team, or has an end date? → Programs & Initiatives (Missions, Partnerships, Events). Everyone contributes here.
2. About a specific product? → Products (its hub).
3. A toolkit, standard, or brand system? → Design Systems.
4. One team's own ongoing work? → that team's folder (Comms, Merch, ID, Facilities, Web & Digital). Open to all.
5. A reusable starting point? → Templates. Retired? → Archive.
